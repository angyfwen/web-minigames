HOW IS THIS EDUCATIONAL?

LIFO Rule

Last-In, First-Out. Just like a burger, the last ingredient you put on is the first one you have to take off to fix a mistake.

Push & Pop

In coding, adding an item is called PUSH. Removing the top item is called POP.

Arrays

Computers store lists in Arrays. They start counting at 0, not 1! Watch the terminal on the right as you play.
