Why is this educational?
The Stack Data Structure: Just like a burger, a "Stack" in coding follows the LIFO rule: Last-In, First-Out.
Push & Pop: Adding an item is called PUSH. Removing the top item is called POP.
Real World: Your computer uses Stacks for things like the "Undo" button and browser history!
